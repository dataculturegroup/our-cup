Caveats and Small Print

I decided to make some simplifying assumptions throughout to make the small project work. For instance, some participating countries are so small that the Census doesn't count them individually. For places like that I've picked a reasonable larger category, which inflates the estimated populaton of fans for that team. This also plays out in the restaurant search and recipes, where I similarly included regional cuisines, or well known ones, to boost the potential for a match.
