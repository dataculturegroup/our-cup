OurCup

America is a nation of immigrants. Find out who lives near you and what teams they'll be supporting in the World Cup. Celebrate their music, food, and culture.

Every four year, the world comes together to celebrate and revel in the joy of soccer. More than half the planet watched the 2018 tournament in Russia.

This year Qatar hosts the men's competition, in their oil-rich kingdom of searing heat. They bribed corrupt FIFA, the sport's global governing body, to win the right to host it.

Immigrant labor has worked years to create 8 new stadiums, with few protections and significant evidence of forced labor in searing 100+ degree temperatures.

But for many of the countries and players, this is the chance of a lifetime. Instead of celebrating the tournament and it human rights tolls, this year let's instead honor and learn about the countries and cultures coming together.

Your neighbors will be tuning in to support their teams and celebrate their culture. Taste their food. Listen to their music. Visit their restaurants. Learn from their citizens. Celebrate their cultures, instead of FIFA's corruption.
